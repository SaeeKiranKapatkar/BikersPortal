About Us:


United Bikers Portal is developed for the purpose of easy communication between world-wide bikers or racers.
Communication and security is a huge problem because, there is always a threat of getting scammed.
That's why, here, you are free to communicate with the seller or the buyer or the partner you are loooking forward to join in a road trip, at your own personal level.
Also, it is all upto you, if you want to share your loaction (buyer) or if you want to share any details of your own.
We suggest, your purchase and delivery should be made in physical modes rather than virtual ones in order to avoid any threats to yourself.
However, we gurantee safety and security to you.
Along the way, we plan to shake hands with a few travel agencies so that you can enjoy your road trips in a well planned and safe manner.
We look forward to involve more and more people in our community.
We hope you have a great time with us.

Frequently Asked Questions:


1) How do I represent myself in potential sellers section?

Ans) You need to fill the form attached in the sellers section in order to represent yourself as a potential seller. Fill the form according to proper instructions. If any instructions are not followed, you will not be identified as a seller.


2) Why can't I find myself in the potential sellers section even after I have filled the form?

Ans) If you have not filled the form completely or if your form is not according to the instructins, either your response will be deleted, or you will be notified through email to fill the form again with proper instructions. If you have filled the form completely and with proper information, kindly wait for a period of 7 days or else send an email regarding the matter to the email mentioned in the contact section of the main page.
